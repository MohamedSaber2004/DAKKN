Fixed: CS1012 "Too many characters in character literal"
========================================================

Root Cause:
Index.cshtml:296 used single quotes inside a Razor @() expression:
    src="@(cat.ImageUrl ?? '/images/placeholders/category-placeholder.svg')"
C# treats single-quoted text as a char literal, not a string.

Fix:
Changed HTML attribute to single quotes and C# string to double quotes:
    src='@(cat.ImageUrl ?? "/images/placeholders/category-placeholder.svg")'
