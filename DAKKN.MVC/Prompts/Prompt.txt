f your goal is product reviews from customers (not a separate testimonial system), and then allowing the admin to choose some customer reviews to appear as testimonials on the landing page, use this prompt:

You are a Senior ASP.NET Core 8 MVC + Clean Architecture + MediatR + Entity Framework Core + ASP.NET Identity + Tailwind CSS engineer working on the DAKKN project.

=================================================
GOAL
=================================================

Implement a complete Product Rating & Review System.

Customers should be able to rate products they purchased and write reviews about their experience.

Admins should be able to manage these reviews and optionally feature selected reviews in the Landing Page Testimonials section.

The system must be fully dynamic and database-driven.

=================================================
BUSINESS FLOW
=================================================

1. Customer purchases a product.

2. After purchase:
   - Customer can submit a rating.
   - Customer can write a review.
   - Customer can edit their review.
   - Customer can delete their review.

3. Product ratings affect:
   - Product Average Rating
   - Product Rating Count

4. Admin can:
   - Approve review
   - Reject review
   - Hide review
   - Delete review
   - Mark review as Featured Testimonial

5. Landing Page:
   - Display only reviews marked as Featured Testimonials.
   - Maximum 3 featured reviews.
   - Completely dynamic.
