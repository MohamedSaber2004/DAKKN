FEATURE: Suggest Your Sticker (End-to-End)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

A customer submits a sticker idea/suggestion (text + optional reference image).
Admins can view suggestions, mark them as Reviewed or Rejected, leave a note,
and optionally convert an approved suggestion into a real Product on the store.

    - Use _unitOfWork.GetRepository<StickerSuggestion>() or IStickerSuggestionRepository

3d. UpdateSuggestionStatusCommandHandler — admin only:
    - Accepts: SuggestionId (Guid), NewStatus (SuggestionStatus), AdminNote (string?),
      ConvertedProductId (Guid? — only when NewStatus = Approved and a product already exists).
    - Call the matching domain method: Approve / Reject / MarkUnderReview / LinkProduct.
    - Guard: only Admin role (checked via ICurrentUserService.UserType).
    - Guard: a Rejected suggestion cannot be re-Approved without going through UnderReview first.
